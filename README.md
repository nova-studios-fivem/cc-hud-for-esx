Hi, I found a very good free hud CC-Hud (https://github.com/CC-Development-I-S/cc-hud) online, but it was only made for QB Core, so I decided to shorten your work and modify it to work for esx as well. 

All credits go to CC-Development.

## VIDEO PREVIEW: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2SeTixG6Lak

## Features
* 9 Unique Preset Designs
* Easily Changeable Logo in Config
* Indicator Light for Vehicle Condition (Red & Orange)
* Changeable Dash Amount and Gap (Splits)
* ... and all features from ps-hud/qb-hud

## Dependencies
* Only QBCore is required

## Installation
* **Remove your current HUD script**
* **Drag and drop cc-hud into your designated resources folder**
* **Configure the script to your liking in the config.lua**
* **ensure it into server.cfg**
